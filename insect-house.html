INSECT HOUSE

Discover nature's little creatures. Encounter a bustling ecosystem of diverse arthropods. From delicate butterflies to the industrious march of ants, each corner unveils a unique display of insect life.

Vibrant beetles, delicate webs hint at the presence of resident spiders, vividly coloured moths and graceful dragon flies and much more can all be spotted at this walk through insect house.

WHERE WILL YOU FIND IT?

The insect house can be found next to the food court.
