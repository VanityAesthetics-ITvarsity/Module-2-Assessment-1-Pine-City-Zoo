ANIMALS MAP PLACES

MONKEY TRAIL

Take an educational stroll on the monkey trail and encounter the friendliest and cutest animals to walk planet earth. The monkey's habitat is safeguarded by a transparent screen, ensuring both conservation and safety. Dispite this protective barrier, their monkey's amiable and inquisitive demeanor remains unmistakably visible, inviting viewers to observe them in their natural surroundings.

WHERE WILL YOU FIND IT?

The monkey trail runs from behind the reception at the main entrance, around the west side of the zoo to the entrance of the lost forest.
